Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

I don't cook very often.
I made this last Wednesday.
This is pretty much how I made it.

Ingrediants

  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Butter
  • Cooked Fettuccine Pasta
  • Minced Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Add and cook garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cream.
  4. Add seasonings.
  5. Simmer sauce.
  6. Add a little bit of pasta water.
  7. Add noodles.
  8. Cook until it feels right.
  9. Done.
